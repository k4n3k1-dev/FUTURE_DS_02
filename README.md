# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis (SaaS Retention Project)

## 🎯 Objective

This project analyzes customer subscription data to understand:
- Why customers leave (churn)
- Which factors influence retention
- How to improve customer lifetime value

This type of analysis is commonly used in SaaS, telecom, and subscription-based businesses.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas (data processing)
- Matplotlib / Seaborn (analysis)
- Streamlit (dashboard)

---

## 📂 Dataset

Customer subscription dataset containing:
- Customer demographics
- Services used
- Contract type
- Monthly and total charges
- Churn status

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

### 📉 Overall Churn Rate
The percentage of customers who left the platform.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📄 Contract Type Impact
- Month-to-month customers have the highest churn rate  
- Long-term contracts significantly improve retention  

---

### 🌐 Internet Service Impact
- Fiber optic users show higher churn compared to DSL users  
- Service type strongly affects retention behavior  

---

### ⏳ Customer Tenure
- Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to churn  
- Long-term customers are significantly more stable  

---

### 💰 Pricing Impact
- Higher monthly charges correlate with increased churn risk  
- Price sensitivity is a key factor in customer loss  

---

## 🚨 Business Problems Identified

- High churn among short-term contract users  
- Pricing pressure on high-paying customers  
- Weak retention in specific service segments  

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Promote yearly and two-year contracts to improve retention  
- Investigate dissatisfaction in high-churn service segments  
- Introduce loyalty benefits for long-term customers  
- Review pricing strategy for high monthly charge plans  

---

## 📊 Dashboard

The Streamlit dashboard includes:
- Overall churn rate KPI  
- Contract-based churn comparison  
- Internet service churn analysis  
- Tenure comparison  
- Monthly charge comparison
